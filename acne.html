Acne & Blemishes

Does your skin look like this?
Then this is for you


Acne - Appearance of blemishes, or small, inflamed bumps, on the surface of the skin
Blemishes - Any type of mark, spot, discoloration, or flaw that appears on the skin


Cleanser

Use the cleanser twice a day(Morning and Night)


The cleanser contains tea tree oil which
is a natural antibacterial with soothing properties
to reduce acne and acne spot.


Toner

After cleanser,you may use the toner.


The toner is a oil-free toner toner which will
reduce bacteria and excess oil and refreshes skin when
when there is excess oil.


Serum

After toner, apply the serum. The serum is optional.


We prefer the serum to be applied for better results on your skin.
If your acne is mild and not extremly bad, then this might be optional for you. But if your skin is breaking out, then serum is a must for your skin.


Moisturizer

After serum/toner ,use the moisturizer.


This water-based light moisturiser acts as
2 in 1 where it can be a primer and moisturiser
for both oily and combination skin.
It provides sunscreen protection against UVA and UVB.
